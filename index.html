About

I wrote my first lines of code over 20 years ago, and I was hooked. Back then, it was all about the thrill of making something work, watching an idea come to life on the screen. But as the years went by, I started asking bigger questions: Why are we building this? Who is it for? How does it fit into the bigger picture?


That curiosity changed my path. I moved from pure development into product ownership, and for the past 6+ years, I have been blending both worlds, building products while shaping the vision behind them.


What drives me now is the challenge of turning complexity into clarity. I love working with teams where hardware, cloud, and data intersect, and helping create the structure that moves a product forward.


For me, product ownership isnt just about managing a backlog. Its about people, conversations, and decisions that make a team stronger and a product truly useful. Thats what keeps me excited every day.
